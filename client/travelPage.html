I have been so fortunate to travel the world and experience the rich cultures of so many different towns, cities and countries. The gift of travel is one of the greatest gifts my parents could have given me and I am privileged to call it one of my life's greatest passions. Every year, we make the yearly pilgrimage to India, my motherland, to revisit the sights and scents of my parents' birthplace. Aside from this, I have also visited such countries as England, France, Greece, Turkey, Mexico, Canada, Czech Republic, Austria, and Germany! I want to continue to cover the world map, collecting anecdotes and experiences, for the rest of my life! Next stop -- winter break in Thailand!
